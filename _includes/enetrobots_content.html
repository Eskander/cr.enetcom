Maya Sacbe

The Maya left us a cultural legacy that we continue to admire and explore today. Proof of this is the “white roads” that we can still find in some archaeological sites such as Chichén Itzá, Cobá, and Dzibilchaltún. To walk along one of these is to literally walk in the footsteps of the Maya and discover an authentic example of their marvellous engineering, along with the commercial and mystic splendor which surrounded them. So are your robots ready to sweep the Maya sacbe! Fasten your belts and let the game start!
